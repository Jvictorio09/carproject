Questions before you book?

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year), a credit card in your name, and a form of ID (passport or national ID). International licenses are accepted in most locations.

Is there a deposit?

Yes, a security deposit is required at pickup. The amount varies by car type (typically $200-$500) and is fully refundable upon return if no damage or violations are found.

What is the fuel policy?

We provide the car with a full tank. You can return it full (no charge) or we'll refuel it for you at market rates plus a small service fee.

Can I cross borders with the car?

Border crossing depends on the rental location and car type. Please inform us in advance, and we'll provide the necessary documentation and approval.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Contact us immediately if your flight is delayed. We'll adjust your pickup time at no extra charge. For significant delays, we may need to reassign your vehicle, but we'll find you an equivalent replacement.

Can someone else drive the car?

Additional drivers can be added for a small daily fee. They must meet the same age and license requirements and be present at pickup to sign the rental agreement.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and debit cards. Corporate accounts can be set up with invoicing options.

How does cancellation work?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee. No-show charges apply if you don't show up without canceling.
